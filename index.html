DUNES
Premiere image Projection / light / ensemble

The first world

Dunes

A 34-minute immersive live experience where five real dune systems shape the music, lighting, and projections.

The landscape is not scenery; it is the structure. Ridges, towers, lagoons, stars, and Reversings each become a musical and spatial world.

  1. I

    Ridges

    Simpson Desert, Australia

    Ancient horizontal dunes stretching unbroken to the horizon.
  2. II

    Towers

    Badain Jaran Desert, China

    The tallest dunes on earth rising beside inland lakes.
  3. III

    Lagoons

    Lençóis Maranhenses, Brazil

    Seasonal rainwater pools inside coastal dunes.
  4. IV

    Stars

    Sahara Desert, Algeria

    Iconic star dunes opening toward the night sky.
  5. V

    Reversings

    Great Sand Dunes, Colorado

    Changing winds pull the work back toward its origin.
