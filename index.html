The company

A studio for long-form instrumental experiences in live rooms.

Return Interval creates concert-scale works that combine composition, electronics, lighting, and projection to transport audiences into carefully shaped musical worlds.

The first world is Dunes: a 34-minute immersive live experience shaped by five real dune systems.

First world

Dunes

A terrain-led performance where landscape becomes structure: ridges, towers, lagoons, stars, and reversings translated into music, light, electronics, and projection.
